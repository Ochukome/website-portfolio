About Me

I’m Favour Ezet a creative who’s always been in tech, just from different angles. I started out doing things like content creation, social media strategy, and virtual assistance, but now I’m diving deeper into software development, and loving every second of it. I currently work as the Social Media Manager at CY Express, where I use strategy and creativity to help brands show up online. I also volunteer with SheCodeAfrica Lagos (which is always so fun!), using my skills to support a community of amazing women in tech. These roles taught me how powerful tech can be for telling stories, building communities, and solving real problems. Now, I’m learning to build those solutions myself — one line of code at a time. I'm especially drawn to frontend development because I love creating things that are not just functional, but also beautiful and user-friendly. When I’m not working or learning, I’m probably watching House MD, singing, dancing, or editing something just for the fun of it I’m passionate, hardworking, and excited to keep growing in this space. If you love tech, good vibes, or want to collaborate — let’s talk!
